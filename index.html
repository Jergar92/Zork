Welcome to Zork: S.O.S: No man's land.

This is a text adventure game based on Zork (http://textadventures.co.uk/games/view/5zyoqrsugeopel3ffhz_vq/zork). This is a University project.

History

Your ship has crashed on an unknown planet, you must retrieve the necessary parts of your destroyed ship scattered around the area and build a beacon for help, your resources are limited and will have to find more to survive.
